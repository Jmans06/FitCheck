Information We Collect
When you use FitCheck, we may collect the following information:

  • User Content: Photos, fashion-related polls, votes, and comments you post within the app.
  • Account Information: Email (if submitted), username, and optional profile details.
  • Usage Data: Interaction data such as votes, views, and app usage patterns.
  • Device Information: Device identifiers, OS version, and app crash logs.
  • Location Data (optional): If enabled, we may use approximate location to improve user experience.

How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:

  • Provide core app functionality, including social polling and voting.
  • Personalize content and improve user experience.
  • Monitor app performance and detect bugs or abuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and ensure safety.

Third-Party Services
We use Google Firebase for:

  • Authentication
  • Realtime database & Firestore
  • Analytics
  • Crash reporting

Firebase may collect device information and usage data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Poll, Content, and Account Deletion
Users can delete their polls or any posted content at any time directly within the app. Users can also delete their account and associated data in-app. Once deleted, this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Data Retention
We retain user data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.
If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed from our systems (excluding any legal exceptions).

Security
We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Children’s Privacy
FitCheck is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store personal data from anyone under this age.

Your Rights
You have control over your content. You may delete polls, content, or your full account anytime in-app.
